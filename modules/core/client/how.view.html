How do payments work

We know you'd ask :) When a brand pays you for your work, we deduct a small percentage of fee from it. The more content you make – hence the more money you earn – the lower your fee is. SproutUp pricing is based on your lifetime earnings.

  • Starter: 8% for the first $1,000 you earn across all campaigns.
  • Pro: 6% for earnings between $1,000.01 and $10,000.
  • Elite: 4% for $10,000.01 and beyond. Forever!
